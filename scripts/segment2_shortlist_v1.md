# Segment 2 — Script Shortlist v1

First batch of 10 Segment 2 scripts (~14–15s spoken each after opener).
`*word*` markers become gold-highlighted caption emphasis in the
rendered video; they are stripped before being sent to ElevenLabs.

The **top 3** picks are listed at the end with rationale.

## Brand opener

Every Segment 2 script opens with one of three locked-in opener
variants (the "Stop doomscrolling" pattern interrupt is the brand
signature). Distribution across the 10 scripts below is random:

- **Opener A** (4× — softer question)
  ```
  Stop *doomscrolling*.
  Have you *prayed* today?
  ```
- **Opener B** (3× — sharper, accusing)
  ```
  Stop *doomscrolling*.
  When's the last time you *prayed*?
  ```
- **Opener C** (3× — command)
  ```
  Stop *doomscrolling*.
  Talk to *God* instead.
  ```

---

## 1. The Storm — Opener B

Stop *doomscrolling*.
When's the last time you *prayed*?
Don't pray for the storm to *pass*.
Pray for *strength* to walk through it.
Every battle? God already knows the *outcome*.
He didn't bring you this *far* to drop you *now*.
Today might be the day He *answers*.

## 2. Counted — Opener A

Stop *doomscrolling*.
Have you *prayed* today?
Feel like God *forgot* you?
He counted every *hair* on your head.
Every tear you've cried — He *caught* it.
Every prayer you whispered — He *heard* it.
You are not *invisible*. You are *His*.

## 3. Chasing — Opener C

Stop *doomscrolling*.
Talk to *God* instead.
You keep *chasing*.
The world will never *fill* you.
That promotion. That *relationship*. That *applause*.
None of it will *complete* you.
Only the One who *made* you can *fulfill* you.

## 4. Be Still — Opener A

Stop *doomscrolling*.
Have you *prayed* today?
One verse can change your *whole day*.
"Be *still*, and know that I am *God*."
Say it. Then say it *again*.
The same God who parted *seas* is fighting for *you*.
So what are you *worried* about?

## 5. Five Minutes — Opener B

Stop *doomscrolling*.
When's the last time you *prayed*?
You don't need more *time*.
You need more *faith*.
Five minutes with God beats five *hours* on this app.
He's not asking for *perfection*. He's asking for *presence*.
Will you give Him *five*?

## 6. Too Far Gone — Opener C

Stop *doomscrolling*.
Talk to *God* instead.
The devil whispers: "you're *too far gone*."
God says: "you're *exactly* who I came for."
Your past doesn't *disqualify* you.
Your scars are *proof* He's still *writing* your story.
Don't put the *pen* down.

## 7. Peace in a Feed — Opener A

Stop *doomscrolling*.
Have you *prayed* today?
You won't find peace in a *feed*.
Real peace isn't *posted* — it's *promised*.
"Peace I leave with you. My peace I *give* to you."
Close the *app*. Open the *Bible*.

## 8. Three Words — Opener C

Stop *doomscrolling*.
Talk to *God* instead.
Don't *overthink* the prayer.
"God, I need you."
Three words. That's the *whole* thing.
He's not waiting for *eloquent*. He's waiting for *honest*.
What's stopping you from saying it *right now*?

## 9. Behind — Opener A

Stop *doomscrolling*.
Have you *prayed* today?
Feel like you're *behind* in life?
Joseph was *thirty* when his dream came true.
Moses was *eighty* when God *called* him.
Abraham had his son at *one hundred*.
God's timing isn't *late*. It's *exact*.

## 10. Testimony — Opener B

Stop *doomscrolling*.
When's the last time you *prayed*?
Today's *struggle* might be tomorrow's *testimony*.
The thing *breaking* you might be the thing *building* you.
God doesn't *waste* pain.
He *redeems* it.
Trust the *process*. He's already in your *future*.

## Top 3 picks (revised)

After the tweaks, **#2 Counted** earned its way into the top 3 over
**#7 Peace in a Feed**. Counted now opens with "Feel like God forgot
you?" — a direct emotional grip — and the triplet (hair / tear / prayer)
plus the closer "You are not invisible. You are His." is the most
screenshot-shareable line in the whole batch.

**#9 — Behind.** Highest viral ceiling. Targets a universal anxiety
(am I falling behind?), uses concrete biblical names with specific
numbers (instantly credible and memorable), and the
Joseph / Moses / Abraham triplet matches the MMO rhythm exactly.
Closer "God's timing isn't *late*. It's *exact*." is the punchline.
**→ wired up as `configs/behind.yaml`.**

**#6 — Too Far Gone.** Highest emotional payoff. Speaks directly to
shame, the dominant emotion driving faith content engagement on IG.
The voice-contrast structure ("devil whispers / God says") is dramatic
and easy to render with caption emphasis. "Your scars are *proof* He's
still *writing* your story. Don't put the *pen* down." is a
quote-card line.

**#2 — Counted.** Most emotionally direct opener of the batch
("Feel like God forgot you?"). Triplet rhythm (hair / tear / prayer)
is tight. Closer "You are not *invisible*. You are *His*." is the
strongest single screenshot line in the shortlist and reads cleanly
as a static caption people will save and share. Best candidate for
the second render.

---

## Honourable mention

**#5 — Five Minutes** got the biggest improvement from the edit pass:
"five hours on this app" (direct fourth-wall call-out) and the
"Will you give Him *five*?" callback to the opening "Five minutes"
turn it from a generic motivator into a cleanly engineered hook→payoff.
Worth queuing for render #3 or #4.
